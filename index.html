Getting Started

Veil for Safari is designed to be simple and intuitive. After installation, you'll see the Veil icon in your Safari toolbar. Click it once to activate and again to deactivate.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable Veil in Safari?

After installation, go to Safari Preferences > Extensions and ensure Veil is enabled. The extension icon should appear in your toolbar.

Does Veil work on all websites?

Veil is designed to work on most standard websites. Some highly dynamic sites or those with unusual structures may have varying results.

Can I customize what gets blurred or removed?

The current version offers a simple on/off toggle that affects the entire page content. Future updates may include more customization options.

Will Veil affect my browsing speed?

Veil is designed to be lightweight and should not significantly impact your browsing experience or speed.

Does Veil collect any data?

No. Veil operates entirely locally on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any user data or browsing information.
